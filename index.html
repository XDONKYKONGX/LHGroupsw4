About LH Group SW Ltd

LH Group SW Ltd has been providing exceptional building and maintenance services across the South West for over 15 years. Founded by Lee Hodges, our company has built a reputation for quality workmanship, reliability, and excellent customer service.

We take pride in our attention to detail and commitment to delivering projects on time and within budget. Whether you need a small repair or a major construction project, you can trust our experienced team to deliver outstanding results.

All our work is fully insured, and we comply with all relevant building regulations and safety standards.
