About LH Group SW Ltd

34 years of excellence in construction services across the South West of England

Our Story

Since 1991, LH Group SW Ltd has been proudly serving the South West of England with unwavering commitment to quality and reliability. Founded by Lee Hodges, our company has grown from a small local business to a trusted name in construction across the region.

With over three decades of experience, we've built a reputation for excellence in every project we undertake. Our journey has been shaped by dedication to craftsmanship, attention to detail, and a customer-first approach that has earned us the trust of clients throughout Devon and Cornwall.

Our Values

At LH Group SW Ltd, our work is guided by core principles that have stood the test of time. These values form the foundation of every project we undertake and every relationship we build.

  • Quality Craftsmanship: We take pride in delivering work of the highest standard
  • Reliability: We honor our commitments and deliver on time, every time
  • Integrity: Honest communication and transparent pricing
  • Customer Focus: Your satisfaction is our ultimate measure of success
  • Innovation: Embracing new techniques while respecting traditional methods

Serving the South West

LH Group SW Ltd proudly serves the entire South West of England, with extensive experience working across Devon, Cornwall, Somerset, and Dorset. Our local knowledge and regional expertise ensure we understand the unique challenges and opportunities of construction in this beautiful part of the country.

From coastal properties facing the elements to inland developments, we have the experience and adaptability to deliver exceptional results regardless of location or project complexity.
