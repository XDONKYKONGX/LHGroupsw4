Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you cover?

We cover the entire South West of England including Devon, Cornwall, Somerset, and Dorset. Our base is in the Torquay area, but we regularly work throughout the region.

How quickly can you start a project?

We typically schedule projects 2-4 weeks in advance, but we can accommodate urgent repairs and emergencies more quickly. Contact us to discuss your timeline.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we provide free, no-obligation estimates for all projects. We'll visit your site, discuss your requirements, and provide a detailed quote.

Are you insured?

Yes, we are fully insured with public liability insurance to give you peace of mind on every project we undertake.
