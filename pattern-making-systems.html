Pattern Making Systems

Precision pattern making and mould fabrication for industrial and commercial applications.

Expert Pattern Making Solutions

At LH Group SW Ltd, we specialize in creating high-precision patterns and moulds for a wide range of industries. Our pattern making expertise combines traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to deliver exceptional results.

Whether you need patterns for concrete casting, composite manufacturing, or specialized industrial applications, our team has the skills and experience to create tools that meet your exact specifications and quality standards.

Decking Molds

Create beautiful, consistent decking products with our custom decking moulds. We design and manufacture precision moulds for composite decking, concrete pavers, and specialized outdoor flooring systems.

Our decking moulds are engineered for durability and precision, ensuring consistent product quality and reducing production costs through efficient manufacturing processes.

  • Custom designs for unique decking patterns
  • Durable construction for high-volume production
  • Precision engineering for consistent results
  • Materials suitable for composite and concrete applications

Construction Molds

Specialized moulds for the construction industry, including concrete formwork, architectural elements, and structural components. Our construction moulds are built to withstand demanding site conditions while maintaining precision.

From decorative concrete panels to structural formwork systems, we create moulds that help construction projects achieve both aesthetic appeal and structural integrity.

  • Architectural concrete moulds
  • Structural formwork systems
  • Custom architectural features
  • Large-scale mould fabrication

Industrial Molds

Precision industrial moulds for manufacturing applications across various sectors. We create durable, high-precision moulds for plastic injection, composite manufacturing, and specialized industrial processes.

Our industrial moulds are engineered for reliability and longevity, helping manufacturers maintain consistent product quality while optimizing production efficiency.

  • Injection moulding tools
  • Composite manufacturing moulds
  • Custom industrial components
  • High-volume production tooling

Business Molds

Custom mould solutions for business applications, including product prototyping, small-batch manufacturing, and specialized commercial products. We help businesses bring their ideas to life with precision tooling.

Whether you're launching a new product or need custom moulds for your business operations, we provide solutions that balance quality, cost-effectiveness, and timely delivery.

  • Product prototyping moulds
  • Small-batch production tooling
  • Commercial product moulds
  • Rapid tooling solutions
