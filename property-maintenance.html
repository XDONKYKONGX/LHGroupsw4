Property Maintenance

Professional maintenance and repair services to keep your property in perfect condition.

Comprehensive Property Care

At LH Group SW Ltd, we provide reliable property maintenance services for homeowners, landlords, and tenants. Our skilled team handles everything from minor repairs to major installations, ensuring your property remains safe, functional, and well-maintained.

With quick response times and quality workmanship, we're your trusted partner for all property maintenance needs across the South West.

New Garage Doors

Upgrade your property with a new garage door that enhances security, improves insulation, and boosts curb appeal. We offer a range of styles from traditional to modern designs.

Our installation service includes precise measurement, professional fitting, and thorough testing to ensure smooth operation and weatherproof performance.

Tenant Move Out Repairs

Prepare your rental property for new tenants with our comprehensive move-out repair service. We address wear and tear, damage, and general maintenance issues efficiently.

Our services include patching and painting walls, repairing flooring, fixing fixtures, and ensuring all appliances are in working order for the next occupants.

Landlord Service Pack

Simplify property management with our comprehensive landlord service package. We offer regular maintenance inspections, emergency repairs, and preventative care.

Our service pack includes quarterly inspections, 24/7 emergency call-out, annual safety checks, and priority scheduling for all maintenance issues.

Leaky Tap Repairs

Stop water waste and prevent damage with our prompt leaky tap repair service. We fix all types of taps, from traditional to modern mixer taps.

Our repair service includes identifying the cause of the leak, replacing worn washers or cartridges, and ensuring proper sealing to prevent future leaks.

New Sink Installation

Upgrade your kitchen or bathroom with a new sink that combines functionality with style. We offer a wide selection of sinks to suit any decor.

Our installation service includes careful removal of the old sink, proper fitting of the new unit, secure plumbing connections, and thorough testing for leaks.

Double Glazing Repairs

Restore the efficiency and appearance of your windows with our professional double glazing repair service. We fix common issues like misted glass, broken seals, and faulty mechanisms.

Our repair services include seal replacement, glass unit replacement, hinge and lock repairs, and draught-proofing to improve energy efficiency.

Front Door Installation

Enhance your home's security, insulation, and curb appeal with a new front door. We offer a variety of styles including uPVC, composite, and traditional wooden doors.

Our installation service includes expert fitting, weatherproofing, and finishing touches to ensure your new door provides security, efficiency, and aesthetic appeal.
