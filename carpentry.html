Carpentry Services

Expert carpentry solutions with precision craftsmanship and attention to detail.

Quality Carpentry Work

At LH Group SW Ltd, we provide professional carpentry services that combine traditional skills with modern techniques. From structural work to fine finishing, our carpenters deliver exceptional results that stand the test of time.

With years of experience and a passion for woodworking, we transform spaces with beautifully crafted carpentry that enhances both functionality and aesthetics.

Stud Walls

Create new room divisions or alter existing layouts with our professional stud wall construction service. We build sturdy, straight partitions that are perfect for creating additional rooms or reconfiguring your space.

Our stud walls are built to precise specifications, properly insulated where required, and finished with plasterboard ready for decorating.

Internal Door Fitting

Upgrade your interior with professionally fitted internal doors that operate smoothly and look beautiful. We offer a wide range of styles from traditional panel doors to modern flush designs.

Our door fitting service includes precise measurement, expert installation, and adjustments to ensure perfect operation and alignment.

External Door Fitting

Enhance your home's security, insulation, and curb appeal with a professionally fitted external door. We install a variety of door types including uPVC, composite, and traditional wooden doors.

Our external door installation includes weatherproofing, secure fitting, and ensuring proper operation for years of reliable service.

Custom Tables

Commission a beautiful, handcrafted table tailored to your specific needs and style preferences. We create dining tables, coffee tables, console tables, and more from quality hardwoods.

Each custom table is built to your exact specifications regarding size, style, and finish, creating a unique centerpiece for your home.

Custom Bed Frames

Create the bedroom of your dreams with a bespoke bed frame crafted to your exact requirements. We design and build bed frames in any size, style, or wood type.

Our custom bed frames include options for integrated storage, headboard designs, and finishes that complement your bedroom decor perfectly.

New Fence Installation

Define your property boundaries and enhance your outdoor space with a professionally installed fence. We offer various styles including panel fencing, closeboard, and picket fences.

Our fencing service includes sturdy post installation, precise alignment, and quality materials that withstand the elements for years to come.
