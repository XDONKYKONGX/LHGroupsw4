GRP Fabrication Experts

At LH Group SW Ltd, we specialize in high-quality Glass Reinforced Plastic (GRP) fabrication for a wide range of applications. Our expertise in composite materials allows us to create durable, lightweight, and corrosion-resistant solutions for both commercial and residential projects.

With state-of-the-art techniques and meticulous attention to detail, we deliver GRP products that offer exceptional performance and longevity in even the most demanding environments.

GRP Roofing

Transform your property with our durable, seamless GRP roofing systems. Glass Reinforced Plastic roofing offers exceptional weather resistance, longevity, and minimal maintenance requirements.

Our GRP roofing installations are custom-formed to your specific requirements, creating watertight seals that withstand the harshest weather conditions while providing excellent thermal performance.

GRP Grip Stems

Enhance safety with our custom-designed GRP grip stems and anti-slip solutions. Perfect for marine environments, industrial settings, and public spaces where secure footing is essential.

Our GRP grip stems are manufactured to exact specifications, offering superior traction, corrosion resistance, and durability in wet or oily conditions.

GRP Mouldings

Create complex shapes and components with our precision GRP moulding service. We produce high-quality mouldings for architectural features, industrial components, and specialized applications.

Our moulding process allows for intricate designs, consistent reproduction, and excellent surface finishes that meet the most demanding specifications.

GRP Dormer Roofs

Upgrade your property with seamless, watertight GRP dormer roofs that enhance both functionality and aesthetics. Our dormer installations provide durable, low-maintenance solutions that blend perfectly with your existing roof.

We specialize in creating custom-formed GRP dormer roofs that offer superior weather resistance and long-term performance without the need for regular maintenance.

Industrial Moulds

Manufacture consistent, high-quality components with our custom industrial mould fabrication service. We create durable moulds for concrete, composites, and various manufacturing processes.

Our industrial moulds are engineered for precision, longevity, and repeated use, helping businesses maintain product consistency and reduce production costs.

GRP Shower Units

Create beautiful, waterproof bathroom solutions with our custom GRP shower units and trays. Our seamless GRP installations prevent leaks and provide easy-to-clean surfaces that resist mold and mildew.

We fabricate shower units to your exact specifications, ensuring perfect fit and finish while offering a wide range of color and texture options to complement your bathroom design.

Our GRP Fabrication Process

1

Design Consultation

We discuss your requirements and create detailed designs for your GRP project.

2

Pattern Making

Our craftsmen create precise patterns or moulds for the GRP fabrication.

3

Material Selection

We select the appropriate resins, glass fibers, and additives for your specific application.

4

Lamination

Our technicians apply layers of resin and glass fiber to create the composite structure.

5

Curing & Finishing

The GRP is cured and finished to your specifications, ready for installation.

Benefits of GRP

Durability

GRP offers exceptional strength and impact resistance, lasting for decades with minimal maintenance.

Water Resistance

Perfect for wet environments, GRP is completely waterproof and resistant to rot and corrosion.

Design Flexibility

GRP can be molded into complex shapes and finished in a wide range of colors and textures.

Lightweight

Despite its strength, GRP is surprisingly lightweight, making installation easier and reducing structural loads.
