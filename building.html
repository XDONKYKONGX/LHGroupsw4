Building Services

Quality Building Solutions

At LH Group SW Ltd, we provide comprehensive building services that combine technical expertise with exceptional craftsmanship. From small renovations to complete home builds, we deliver quality results that stand the test of time.

Our experienced team handles every aspect of construction with precision and attention to detail, ensuring your project is completed to the highest standards.

Extensions

Expand your living space with a beautifully designed extension that seamlessly integrates with your existing property. We handle everything from planning permission to completion.

Whether you need extra bedroom space, a larger kitchen, or a sunroom, our team will create a functional and attractive extension that adds value to your home.

Walls

From structural walls to decorative features, we provide expert wall construction services using the highest quality materials and techniques.

Our services include new wall construction, wall repairs, retaining walls, and feature walls that enhance both the interior and exterior of your property.

Complete Home Build

Realize your dream home with our complete home building service. We manage the entire process from initial design to final finishing touches.

Our team works closely with you to create a custom home that reflects your lifestyle and preferences, ensuring every detail meets your expectations.

French Doors Fitting

Enhance your home's natural light and accessibility with expertly fitted French doors. We provide professional installation that ensures perfect operation and weatherproofing.

Our French door installation service includes careful measurement, precise fitting, and finishing touches that create a seamless transition between indoor and outdoor spaces.

Window Enlargement

Transform your living spaces with our window enlargement services. Increase natural light and improve views by professionally enlarging existing window openings.

We handle structural modifications with precision, ensuring the integrity of your property while creating brighter, more inviting interiors.
