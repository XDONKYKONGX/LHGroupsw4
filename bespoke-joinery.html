Bespoke Joinery

Transforming spaces with exquisite, custom-crafted woodwork designed uniquely for you and your home.

Master Craftsmanship in Wood

At LH Group SW Ltd, we believe that exceptional joinery is the heart of a beautiful home. Our master craftsmen combine traditional techniques with modern precision to create stunning, functional pieces that elevate your living space.

Each project is meticulously planned and crafted to your exact specifications, ensuring a perfect blend of form and function that reflects your personal style.

Custom Windows

Our bespoke windows are crafted to enhance both the aesthetics and functionality of your home. We create beautiful, energy-efficient windows that perfectly complement your property's architecture.

Using premium materials and precise craftsmanship, we ensure each window provides excellent insulation, security, and durability while maximizing natural light and views.

Custom Internal Doors

Elevate your interior spaces with our beautifully crafted internal doors. We design and create doors that perfectly match your home's style while providing smooth functionality.

From traditional panel designs to contemporary flush styles, our internal doors are built to the highest standards, ensuring they operate flawlessly for years to come.

Custom External Doors

Make a lasting impression with our handcrafted external doors. We create stunning entryways that provide security, weather protection, and exceptional curb appeal.

Our external doors are built to withstand the elements while maintaining their beauty. We use only the finest materials and advanced joinery techniques to ensure longevity and performance.

Custom Staircases

Transform your home with a stunning custom staircase that serves as both a functional element and a striking architectural feature.

Our craftsmen design and build staircases that perfectly fit your space and style, whether you prefer classic elegance or modern minimalism. Each staircase is engineered for safety and built to last.

Custom Wardrobes

Maximize your storage space with our beautifully crafted custom wardrobes. We design storage solutions that perfectly fit your space, needs, and aesthetic preferences.

From walk-in dressing rooms to fitted bedroom wardrobes, we create functional and elegant storage solutions with attention to every detail, including interior organization systems.

Custom Tables

Create a focal point for your living or dining space with a beautifully crafted custom table. We design and build tables that become the centerpiece of your room.

Whether you need a dining table, coffee table, or console table, we work with you to create a unique piece that perfectly suits your space, style, and functional requirements.
